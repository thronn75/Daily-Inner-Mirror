# Daily Inner Mirror
A 10-second daily check-in that reflects your emotional trend — without journaling, without diagnoses, without advice.

## What it does
- **One daily question:** “How are you, really?”
- Choose one option:
  - Good
  - Okay
  - Heavy
- See your **7-day and 30-day trends** at a glance.

## Designed to feel safe
- Minimal UI
- No feed
- No social
- No pressure to write or explain

## Privacy
Daily Inner Mirror is built with privacy in mind.
- Data stays on your device (unless you enable iCloud sync, if available in your build).
- No selling of personal data.
- No advertising.
