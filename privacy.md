# Privacy Policy — Daily Inner Mirror
**Effective date:** {date}

Daily Inner Mirror is designed to be private by default. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

## Summary
- **We do not sell your data.**
- **We do not use third-party advertising SDKs.**
- Your check-ins are intended to stay **on your device** (unless you enable optional sync features, if available).

## Data you provide
Daily Inner Mirror may store the following information:
- Your daily selection (e.g., Good / Okay / Heavy)
- The date/time of your check-in
- App preferences (e.g., reminders, display settings), if used

This data is used only to show your trends and app functionality.

## Data we collect automatically
We do **not** collect personal identifiers (like name, address, or contacts).

If the app includes optional diagnostics (crash logs), it may collect:
- crash reports
- basic device and OS information
This is used only to improve stability and performance.

## Analytics
Daily Inner Mirror does not require analytics to function.  
If analytics is included in your build, it should be **privacy-friendly** and collect only minimal, non-identifying usage information.

## iCloud / Sync (optional)
If your version supports iCloud sync and you enable it:
- Your check-ins may be stored in your iCloud account
- Apple processes this data according to Apple’s iCloud terms and privacy policies

## Data retention
Your check-ins remain stored until you:
- delete them in the app (if supported), or
- delete the app (removes local data), and/or
- disable iCloud sync (if used)

## Children’s privacy
Daily Inner Mirror is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Contact
If you have questions about privacy:
Email: **support@yourdomain.com**

## Changes to this policy
We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.
